Partner Q&A

Hiring & Compliance Partnership FAQs

Get immediate, detailed corporate answers regarding local Employer of Record setups, software access permissions, and billing systems.

What is the Employer of Record (EOR) hiring structure?

An Employer of Record (EOR) is a legal framework where Dexton Solvexx employs professionals on your behalf in their local country. We handle local labor compliance, generate compliant employment contracts, process local currency payroll, withhold taxes, and administer statutory benefits. You maintain 100% daily task management and project delivery control over the employee.

How do you secure candidate systems access and data privacy?

We implement highly strict data security guidelines. We provision high-performance company laptops pre-configured with Mobile Device Management (MDM) software, enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA), establish secure VPN setups, and configure encrypted local storage systems. Offshore staff operate from secure workspaces, protecting your corporate repositories.

How is the monthly workforce billing structured?

We deliver a single, unified monthly invoicing statement. This invoice covers candidate local base salary, statutory taxes, local social security contributions, supplemental benefits (health/pension), and our flat monthly management fee. There are no hidden fees or transactional setup penalties, giving you complete visibility into global staffing budgets.
