Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Dexton Solvexx (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, share, and protect your personal data when you visit our website, submit recruitment inquiries, or engage with our Employer of Record (EOR) and global staffing services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect personal data to deliver professional global staffing and recruitment services. This data includes:

  • Contact Information: Full name, corporate email, phone number, and physical office addresses.
  • Recruitment Information: Resume documents, portfolio files, academic history, licensing credentials, and reference logs.
  • Operational Data: System logs, device identifiers, and connection metrics when accessing our secure portals.

3. How We Process Data

We process personal data in strict compliance with international regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, and CCPA). We use this data to:

  • Pre-screen, technically evaluate, and place candidates with target international enterprises.
  • Manage local currency payrolls, tax withholdings, EOR compliance, and statutory benefits.
  • Secure corporate workspaces, laptops, VPN credentials, and prevent unauthorized system transfers.

4. Data Protection & Security

We deploy robust administrative, physical, and technological security controls (including ISO 27001 parameters, secure databases, encrypted transport, and endpoint device management) to protect data from unauthorized access or leakage.
